Avocado Tomato Bruschetta

Serves 1

Vegan

Ingredients

  • 6 chopped cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 sliced avocado
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • Finely chopped fresh basil
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix the cherry tomatoes with the balsamic vinegar in a bowl.
  2. Place the avocado onto the bread then add the tomatoes an garlic.
  3. Finally, add some basil, salt and pepper to taste.

Squash Soup

Serves 4

Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1/2 fresh red chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp ground corriander
  • 1 small buternut squash, peeled and chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 x 400g tin coconut milk
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the cocnut oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat and gently fry the diced onion for about 5 mins.
  2. Add the garlic, chilli, cumin, thyme and corriander and fry for another minute.
  3. Pour in 1 litre boiling water, add the squash, carrots and coconut milk and simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and whizz in a blender untill smooth

Arancini

Serves 4

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 30g butter
  • 200g mushrooms
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 300g arborio rice
  • 800ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 125g buffalo mozarella, cut into 12 pieces
  • 4tbsp plain flour, seasoned
  • 3tbsp suflower oil

Instructions

  1. Heat 20g butter in a fyring pan over medium heat, add the mushrooms and fry for 4 mins, then set aside.
  2. Return the pan to the heat, add the onion, garlic and remaining butter and cook for 5 mins.
  3. Add the rice and stir for a minute, then add the vegetable stock a ladeful at a time until nearly absorbed, kepp adding until the rice is cooked - about 20 mins.
  4. Add the mushrooms and thyme. Season, cool slightly, then chill until cold.
  5. Shape into 12 balls, adding a piece of mozarella in the centre. Then roll each one in flour.
  6. Heat the sunflower oil in a large frying pan, then fry them for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden.
