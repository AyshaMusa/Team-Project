Avocado Tomato Bruschetta

Serves 1

Vegan

Ingredients

  • 6 chopped cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 sliced avocado
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • Finely chopped fresh basil
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix the cherry tomatoes with the balsamic vinegar in a bowl.
  2. Place the avocado onto the bread then add the tomatoes an garlic.
  3. Finally, add some basil, salt and pepper to taste.

Squash Soup

Serves 4

Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1/2 fresh red chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp ground corriander
  • 1 small buternut squash, peeled and chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 x 400g tin coconut milk
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the cocnut oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat and gently fry the diced onion for about 5 mins.
  2. Add the garlic, chilli, cumin, thyme and corriander and fry for another minute.
  3. Pour in 1 litre boiling water, add the squash, carrots and coconut milk and simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and whizz in a blender untill smooth

Vegetarian
