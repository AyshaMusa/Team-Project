Avocado Tomato Bruschetta

Vegan!

Serves 1

Preparation time: 10 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients Measure
Cherry tomatoes 6, chopped
Balsamic vinegar 1 teapoon
Avocado 1/2
Garlic 1 clove, crushed
Fresh basil 1 handfull
Sea salt a pinch
Black pepper a pinch

Method

  1. Mix the cherry tomatoes with the balsamic vinegar in a bowl.
  2. Place the avocado onto the bread then add the tomatoes an garlic.
  3. Finally, add some basil, salt and pepper to taste.

Squash Soup

Vegan!

Serves 4

Preparation time: 20 mins

Cooking time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients Measure
Coconut oil 1 teaspoon
Onion 1, diced
Garlic 1 clove, crushed
Fresh red chilli 1, deseeded and chopped
Ground cummin 1 teaspoon
Dried thyme 1 teaspoon
Ground corriander 1/2 teaspoon
Buuternut squash 1 small, peeled and chopped
Carrots 2, peeled and chopped
Coconut milk 1/2 x 400g
Sea salt a pinch
Black pepper a pinch

Method

  1. Heat the cocnut oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat and gently fry the diced onion for about 5 mins.
  2. Add the garlic, chilli, cumin, thyme and corriander and fry for another minute.
  3. Pour in 1 litre boiling water, add the squash, carrots and coconut milk and simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and whizz in a blender untill smooth

Nachos

Vegetarian!

Serves 4

Preparation time: 15 mins

Cooking time: 10 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients Measure
Peppers 2
Fresh red chilli 1
Tomatoes 3
Spring onions 6
Fresh corriander 1 bunch
Limes 2
Olive oil 1 tablespoon
Corn tortillas 4
Cumin seeds 1/2 teaspoon
Chipotle Tabasco sauce 1 tablespoon
Black beans 1 x 400g tin
Avocado 1
Feta cheese 20g

  4. Add the diced tomatoes and spring onions in another bowl, then add the diced chilli and pepper.

  6. Cut the tortillas into wedges and arrange over two baking sheets. Bake in the oven for 5 minutes, or until golden.
  7. Place a large frying pan over a high heat and toast the cumin seeds for a few seconds. Add the Tabasco sauce and beans and cook for a few minutes, or until thick, stirring occasionally.
  8. Peel, destone and slice the avocado into wedges, then drizzle with the remaining lime juice.
