    • Chickpea and Coriander Burger

    Can be made Vegan!

    Serves 4

    Preparation time: 15 mins

    Cooking time: 10 mins

    Ingredients Measure
    Tinned chickpeas 1 can, drained
    Lemon zest 1, plus juice
    Ground cumin 1 teaspoon
    Fresh coriander 1 small bunch, chopped
    Egg (or vegan egg replacer) 1
    Fresh breadcrumbs 100g
    Red onion 1 medium, half diced, half sliced
    Olive oil 1 tablespoon
    Burger buns of choice 4
    Tomato 1 large, sliced

    Method

    1. In a food processor, whizz the chickpeas, lemon zest, lemon juice, cumin, half the coriander, the egg and some seasoning.
    2. Scrape into a bowl and mix with 80g of the breadcrumbs and the diced onions.
    3. Form 4 burgers, press remaining breadcrumbs onto both sides and chill for at least 10 mins.
    4. Heat the oil in a frying pan until hot. Fry the burgers for 4 mins each side, keeping the heat on medium so they donâ€™t burn.
    5. To serve, slice each bun and fill with a slice of tomato, a burger, a few red onion slices, and any other garnishes of choice.
