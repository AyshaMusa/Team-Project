Kind Cooking is a website all about celebrating what can be done with plant based ingredients!

Some people are drawn to vegetarianism because they believe it will help them live a longer, healthier life. Others have made the switch to preseve Earth's natural resources or from a love of animals and an ethical opposition to eating them.

Recent scientific research has demonstrated the health and environmental benefits of a plant-based diet, even the federal government recommends that we consume most of our calories from grain products, vegetables and fruits.

But whether you're veggie or not, there are numerous delicious recipes on here to try!
