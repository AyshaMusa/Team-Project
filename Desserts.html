Pancakes

Vegan friendly!

Makes 6-8 pancakes

Preparation time: 5 mins

Cooking time: 1-2 mins per pancake

IngredientsMeasure
Flour (any kind)1 cup
Sugar1 tablespoon
Baking Powder2 tablespoons
Soymilk1 cup
Vegetable oil2 tablespoons

Method

  1. Set pan on stove at medium heat.
  2. Combine the 3 dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder) in a bowl.
  3. Add the soymilk and vegetable oil and mix until smooth.
  4. Poor or spoon one pancake's worth of mix into the pan.
  5. Flip pancake when bubbles appear in the middle and/or if the edges are looking stiffened.
  6. Add toppings of your choice or eat without!

Avacado and Strawberry Ices

Vegan friendly!

Serves 4

Preparation time: 5 mins

IngredientsMeasure
Ripe strawberries200g
Avacado1
Balsamic vinegar2 teaspoons
Vanilla extract1/2 teaspoon
Maple syrup (optional)1-2 teaspoons

Method

  1. Chop strawberries.
  2. Stone, peel and chop avocado.
  3. Put all ingredients (save four pieces of strawberry for decoration) into a bowl and blitz using a blender or food processor.
  4. Once smooth, taste and add maple syrup to sweeten to preference.
  5. Poor mix into four containers.
  6. Adda strawberry for decoration to each container.
  7. Cover with clingfilm and freeze.
  8. Allow each to soften for 5-10 mins before eating.

Tiffin

Vegan friendly!

Makes 25 pieces

Preparation time: 15 mins

Baking time: 3 mins

IngredientsMeasure
Coconut oil75g (plus extra for greasing tin)
Vegan dark chocolate200g
Golden syrup2 tablespoons
Vegan ginger nuts200g
Dried cranberries100g
Toasted pistachios50g

Method

  1. Lightly oil a 20cm square tin with coconut oil.
  2. Line the base of the tin with baking paper.
  3. Chop dark chocolate and pistachios.
  4. Melt together chocolate, maple syrup and coconut oil in the microwave for 30 second bursts until mixture is smooth.
  5. Break the ginger nuts into small pieces and add, with dried cranberries and pistachios into the melted mixture.
  6. Combine melted mixture with dry ingredients.
  7. Spoon into the tin.
  8. Place in fridge for 2 hours or until mixture sets hard.
  9. Once set, cut into 25 small square pieces.
